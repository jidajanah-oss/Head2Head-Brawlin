# Head2Head Brawlin – Official League Rules

## League Overview

Head2Head Brawlin is a season-long NFL Pick'em league built around head-to-head competition. Each player represents one unique NFL team.

---

# League Membership

* Maximum of 32 players.
* Each player is assigned one unique NFL team.
* No two players may own the same NFL team.
* Players are managed by the Commissioner.

---

# Weekly Picks

* Players make picks for every NFL game.
* Picks for each game lock **5 minutes before that game's scheduled kickoff**.
* Games that have already locked cannot be changed.
* Sunday afternoon games remain editable until 5 minutes before their individual kickoff times.
* Sunday Night, Monday Night, and other standalone games remain editable until 5 minutes before those games begin.

---

# Scoring

* Win = 3 points
* Tie = 1 point
* Loss = 0 points

Season standings are based on total points.

---

# Head-to-Head Matchups

Each week, league members compete in scheduled head-to-head matchups.

Matchups are generated according to league scheduling rules.

---

# Player Visibility

Before picks lock:

* Players may view only their own picks.

After a game's picks lock:

* Picks for that game become visible according to league settings.

---

# Commissioner Powers

The Commissioner can:

* Add, edit, or remove players.
* Assign NFL teams.
* Open or close weekly picks.
* Manage league rules.
* Manage schedules.
* Publish announcements.
* Correct league data when necessary.
